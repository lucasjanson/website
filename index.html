Curriculum Vitae

I am a Professor of Statistics and Affiliate in Computer Science at Harvard University, where I study high-dimensional inference and statistical machine learning.

In addition to my methodological and theoretical work, I collaborate with domain experts to apply my work to scientific fields including genetics, political science, and climatology, and I run the Harvard Statistical Consulting Service where I supervise PhD students advising hundreds of researchers per year.
